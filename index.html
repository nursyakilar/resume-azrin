About

A career built on audits, evidence & education

"To leverage my Ts. certification and MSPO auditing expertise in a role that advances sustainability and regulatory compliance within the palm oil industry."

I holds a Degree in Biology from Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) and has spent over six years working across Sabah's palm oil sector — first as a Plantation Advisor and MSPO Coordinator, and today as Managing Director of two consultancy and training firms, GTA Consultancy and FMJ Management Academy PLT.

My work sits at the intersection of three disciplines which are environmental science (biodiversity assessments, GIS mapping, carbon footprint accounting), management systems auditing (MSPO, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 14064), and corporate training — having delivered MSPO and compliance training to collection centres, estates and dealers across the region.

I am also an Operation Manager overseeing three private schools, where I apply the same systems-thinking to STEM education, safety and sustainability programmes.
